### Minimum specs (for running 2–3 VMs):
- CPU: 4 cores (i5/Ryzen 5 or better)
- RAM: 16 GB (8 GB possible with care)
- Storage: 256–500 GB SSD
- OS: Windows/Linux/Mac (any)
